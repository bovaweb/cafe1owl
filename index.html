Our Story

A corner of Kolkata that never hurries

Tucked behind Golpark on Purna Das Road, Wise Owl began as a dream — a place where morning joggers, daydreaming students, and evening couples could all feel at home. Over a decade later, that dream still flickers under a canopy of fairy lights.

Signature

⭐ Signature
Joey's Platter
The quintessential Wise Owl breakfast. Eggs your way, crispy bacon, grilled sausages, sautéed mushrooms and golden toast.
Eggs Benedictine
Poached eggs on toasted muffins with velvety hollandaise — a weekend ritual for regulars.
Fluffy Pancake Stack
Thick buttermilk pancakes with maple, fresh berries and whipped cream. Morning indulgence, redefined.
Overnight Oats & Granola
For the health-conscious early bird. Cold-soaked oats with seasonal fruits and house-made granola.
⭐ Chef's Pride
Chicken Rosemary Steak
Tender chicken breast in rosemary-garlic marinade, pan-seared to perfection. The dish that made Wise Owl's rooftop legendary.
⏳ Made fresh — allow 35–45 min
⭐ Local Favourite
Grilled Bhetki
Fresh Bhetki fillet grilled whole with a bright lemon butter sauce. A Kolkata original with continental elegance.
⏳ Made fresh — allow 30–40 min
Spaghetti Aglio e Olio
House-made pasta tossed with golden garlic, chilli flakes, parsley and premium olive oil.
Continental Chicken Stew
Slow-simmered vegetables and chicken in a rich herb broth. The dish that feels like a hug.
Seekh Kebab
Hand-minced chicken with caramelized onions and aromatic spices, charcoal-grilled. Served with mint chutney.
